ABOUT ME

I am an engineer based in Brisbane, Australia. While more skilled in some areas than others, I consider myself a generalist rather than a specialist at any one thing.

I like to study, tinker and design in a wide variety of disciplines. Here are some technical fields I am interested and involved in:

  • Automation and control systems- from UAVs to home automation
  • Energy and sustainability
  • Linux and open source computing
  • Software development in python
  • Statistics, data analysis and machine learning
  • Quantatitve finance and investing, algorithmic trading, active portfolio management
  • Fluid mechanics, thermodynamics and their applications
  • Cryptography and penetration testing
  • Tutoring and teaching- from structural engineering to computer science to mathematics and physics

I am passionate about technology and its implementation in problem solving. I love to work both in a team environment and independantly, building a solution in the most efficient way possible. The icon next to my name is of Daedalus constructing wings for his son, Icarus. The story of Daedalus embodies many of the things that inspire me (apart from him possibly murdering his nephew and rival, of course). He was a skilled innovator, and broke free from the chains of tyranny against impossible odds.

Outside of science and technology, I also have other interests:

  • Hanging out with my fiance Rikki
  • Travelling
  • Flying
  • Snorkelling and spearfishing
  • Camping
  • Consuming copious amounts of alcohol with my mates
  • Advocating sustainability and equality

If you want to define me by what I'm good at, as some people do, here's a few things:

  • Project engineering- experienced in planning, managing, designing, installing and upgrading building management and security control systems
  • Computer systems administration- Windows, Linux and vSphere
  • Database management- SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL and SQLite
  • Networking- not just TCP/IP, other protocols, such as BACnet
  • Scientific computing- writing basic programs in languages such as python, R or Matlab to solve or assist in solving real-world problems
  • Tutoring- experienced in high-school and tertiary level mathematics, statistics, engineering and phyics subjects
